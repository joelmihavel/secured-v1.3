Landlord Onboarding and Rental Income Protection Terms
Service Provider: Slaash Technologies Private Limited (trading as "Flent" & "Flent Secured")
1. Introduction & Service Overview

Welcome to Flent Secured — a modern rental management platform designed to bring security and simplicity to your rental income.

What We Provide
  • Rental Management: streamlined payments, verification, and documentation.
  • Income Protection: financial guarantee against vacancy & tenant default.
  • Payment Coordination: secure rent settlement via RBI-regulated partners.
  • Verifiable Records: auditable payment history.
What This Covers
  • Enrollment in Flent Secured rental management.
  • Rental Income Protection Plan terms.
  • Payment processing arrangements.
  • Property and tenancy verification protocols.

Fee Structure: This service is currently provided at no cost. Should pricing be introduced, you will receive 30 days' advance notice with the option to continue or discontinue.

2. Your Consent & Authorization

By proceeding, you confirm and authorize the following:

2.1 Tenancy Verification — You authorize Flent Secured to verify that the tenant who invited you actually resides in your property, the existence and condition of the property, and tenancy details including rent amount, lease duration, and payment history.

2.2 Payment Processing — You authorize Slaash Technologies Private Limited to facilitate rent payments through our platform, coordinate processing via RBI-regulated payment gateways, arrange direct settlement to your registered bank account, and maintain comprehensive transaction records.

2.3 Platform Usage — You agree to keep your property listed on Flent Secured for the protection period, process all rent payments from enrolled tenants through the platform, provide accurate property and bank details, and respond to verification requests within reasonable timeframes.

3. Rental Income Protection Plan

This guarantee protects against two critical rental income risks and is backed by Slaash Technologies Private Limited through institutional risk management partnerships.

Un-occupancy Guarantee
  • If vacant 30+ days after tenant moves out and Flent can't find a replacement, you receive up to one month's rent (max ₹1,50,000).
  • Tenant must serve 30-day notice via Flent.
  • Property must remain listed & available.
Tenant Abandonment
  • If tenant abandons and property stays vacant beyond the security deposit adjustment period (min 60 days), you receive up to one month's rent (max ₹1,50,000).
  • Abandonment must be verified via Flent.

Activation Requirements:

  1. Minimum 3 rent payments completed through Flent.
  2. 90-day waiting period from enrollment date.
  3. All subsequent rent payments processed via Flent.
  4. Tenant identity & address verification completed.
  5. Property remains listed throughout the period.
4. Payment Processing & Settlement
  • Tenant initiates payment through Flent Secured using their preferred method.
  • Processed via authorized RBI-regulated payment gateways.
  • Settled directly to your registered bank account (typically within 1–2 business days).
  • Auditable records accessible 24/7 via your dashboard — verifiable proof of income for tax compliance.
5. Property & Tenant Verification
Property
  • Geo-tagged photography
  • Ownership/rental agreement review
  • Utility bill verification
  • Remote or in-person verification
Tenant
  • Identity (PAN & Aadhaar e-KYC)
  • Address verification
  • Tenancy confirmation
6. Your Obligations
  • Information Accuracy: Maintain current property/bank details; update within 7 days of changes.
  • Platform Exclusivity: Process all rent exclusively through Flent Secured.
  • Tenant Cooperation: Respond to placement opportunities within 48 hours; notify of vacancy/abandonment within 24 hours.
  • Continuity: Do not remove property while coverage is active.
7. Claims Process
  1. Notify: Submit claim within 7 days via platform or support@flentsecured.com.
  2. Document: Provide move-out notice, timeline, communications, and availability proof.
  3. Verify: Cooperate with KYC, ownership, and tenant verification.

Timeline: Initial Review (5–7 days) → Verification (7–10 days) → Payout (10–15 days). Total 15–30 business days.

8. Liability, Privacy & Compliance

Coverage Limits: Maximum ₹1,50,000 per event and per policy year. We do not guarantee placement timeframes, assume maintenance responsibilities, or provide legal advice.

Privacy: Your data is processed to provide services, verify eligibility, process claims, and comply with RBI payment regulations. Data shared only with essential payment processors, coverage partners, or regulatory authorities.

Regulatory Compliance: Flent Secured operates in full compliance with RBI regulations. You remain responsible for your independent tax obligations (income reporting, TDS under Section 194-I, etc.).

9. General Terms & Termination

Termination: Coverage ends if you remove the property, stop processing rent via Flent, provide false information, or violate material terms.

Governing Law: Laws of India. Exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Bangalore, Karnataka.
